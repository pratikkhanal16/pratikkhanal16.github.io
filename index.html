I am a hydrology researcher specializing in the intersection of climate change, artificial intelligence, and water resources management. My work bridges advanced computational modeling with real-world impact.

Focused on Glacial Lake Outburst Flood (GLOF) modeling, hydrodynamic simulations, and machine learning-driven hydro-meteorological systems, I develop tools that help communities prepare for an increasingly uncertain climate future.
