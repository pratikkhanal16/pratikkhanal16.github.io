01 — Profile

The Science of
Moving Water

Currently pursuing a Master's in Water Engineering and Management at the Asian Institute of Technology, Thailand, Pratik works at the intersection of classical hydraulics and modern AI-driven data systems.


His field research spans the Himalayas to urban watersheds, contributing to flood hazard mapping, sediment monitoring, and river training structures across Nepal. His academic record places him among the top civil engineers at Tribhuvan University.


Registered as a Civil Engineer (No. 86044 "Civil A") with Nepal Engineering Council since October 2024.
